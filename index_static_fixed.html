FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Are you professionals?+–

We're a student-run remote support team. We take it seriously, but this is also how we learn real IT by helping the community. For advanced issues, we'll recommend a trusted adult technician.

Do you charge money?+–

We post clear prices for common help. For bigger projects we quote by the hour and always ask before doing paid work.

Can you help remotely?+–

Yes—our sessions are online-only and may use safe screen-sharing (with a parent/guardian involved). We do not offer in-person visits.

What do you need from me?+–

Describe the problem, your device type, and what you've already tried. If you're under 18, please have a parent/guardian involved.
